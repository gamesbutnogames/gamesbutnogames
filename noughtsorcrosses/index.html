How To Play

Rather than place down pieces, halves are placed down
Halves can be flipped before being placed down
Two halves when placed next to each other may make a nought or a cross
After all spaces on the board are filled, take turns flipping pieces already on the board
Either player can attempt to make crosses or noughts, the colours of the pieces just show whose turn it is
Make a line of four full pieces without your opponent making one to win
