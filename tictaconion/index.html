How To Play

Complete a line of 4 of your own onions to win

You can move any of your own onions on your turn to a valid position on the grid

Onions can be moved to empty grid spaces, or on top of smaller onions

For a winning line ot be valid, your onion must be the largest on each of the grid spaces making up the line
